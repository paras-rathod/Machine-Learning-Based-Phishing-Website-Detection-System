About Phishing

Phishing is an attempt to obtain sensitive information such as username, passwords and credit card details often for malicious reasons by disguising as a trusworthy entity in an electronic communication.

{{ error }}

Here are few tips to prevent a victim of phishing

  1. Guard against spam.
  2. communicate personal information only via phone or secure websites.
  3. Do not click on links, download files or open attachments in email form unknown senders.
  4. Never email personal or financial information, even if you are close with the recipient.
  5. Beware of links in email that ask for personal information.
  6. Protect your computer with a firewall, spam filters, anti-virus and anti-spyware softwares.
  7. Never enter personal information in a pop up screen.
